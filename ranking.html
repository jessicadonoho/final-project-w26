NPR Ranking

  • Up First
  • Summary: Quick daily news covering top U.S. and global stories.

  • Genre: News, Daily Briefing
  • Hosts: Rotating (Steve Inskeep, Leila Fadel, A Martínez)

  • NPR Politics Podcast
  • Summary: Breakdown of current U.S. politics in a conversational format.
  • Average Length: 15–25 minutes
  • Genre: Politics, News
  • Hosts: NPR political reporters (Tamara Keith, Domenico Montanaro)

  • It's Been a Minute
  • Summary: Culture, news, and social issues with interviews and commentary.
  • Average Length: 30–50 minutes
  • Genre: Culture, Society
  • Host: Brittany Luse

  • NPR News Now
  • Summary: Hourly news updates in a fast, headline format.
  • Average Length: ~5 minutes
  • Genre: News
  • Host: NPR newsroom

  • Code Switch
  • Summary: Deep dives into race, identity, and culture.
  • Average Length: 30–40 minutes
  • Genre: Society, Culture
  • Hosts: Gene Demby, Shereen Marisol Meraji

  • Consider This
  • Summary: One major story explained in depth each weekday.
  • Average Length: 10–15 minutes
  • Genre: News Analysis
  • Hosts: Rotating NPR journalists
